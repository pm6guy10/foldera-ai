# Vision

## The Facebook pixel, inverted

The Facebook pixel is the world's most proven total-context machine. It ingests
everything about a person from their digital exhaust — every signal, every site,
every behavior — builds a model, and hits them at exactly the right moment with
exactly the right thing. It works, at planetary scale, every second.

Foldera is the same machine with the arrow flipped:

- The pixel uses total context → **to sell you something.**
- Foldera uses total context → **to look out for you.**

Same problem, sign changed. The technology is proven. The guardian is not science
fiction.

## What Foldera does

Foldera is a **Workday Presence Layer**: it finds your workday re-entry point
*before* you reopen five tools, then gives you **one grounded next move** — or
stays quiet.

- Connects to your work sources (Gmail, Calendar, Drive, Microsoft).
- Reconstructs context from what actually changed.
- Surfaces a single safe next move at a time, with a receipt.
- Stays silent when no move is justified.

It exists so you never have to rebuild your day every time you reopen your tools.

## What Foldera is not

- Not a dashboard, task manager, inbox summarizer, or chatbot UI.
- Not surveillance or screen-reading.
- Not a broad workflow-automation suite or connector theater.
- Not a product that needs its founder to narrate every next step.

The whole point is **one intervention at a time**, grounded in real sources, that
you approve or don't.
