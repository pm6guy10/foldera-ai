An AI executive assistant should do more than answer prompts. It should reduce operational drag by detecting what needs attention, preparing the work, and helping decisions move faster.

That means its value is not just language generation. Its value is context, prioritization, and timing. Without those, it behaves more like a writing tool than an assistant.
