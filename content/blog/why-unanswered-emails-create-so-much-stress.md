Unanswered emails are stressful because they combine uncertainty with obligation. You know the thread still exists, but you do not know whether it needs action now, whether the delay is normal, or what the right response should be.

That uncertainty creates mental carry cost. Even when you are not looking at the email, it is still taking up cognitive space because the loop is not closed.
