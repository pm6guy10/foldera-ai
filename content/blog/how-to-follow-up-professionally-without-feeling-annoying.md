Good follow up is not about being more polite. It is about having a real reason for the follow up and making the next step easier for the other person. The more specific and grounded the message, the less it feels like empty pressure.

What makes follow up feel awkward is usually vagueness. If you are only reaching out to check in, the message often feels weak even before you send it. A concrete ask or decision point changes that.
