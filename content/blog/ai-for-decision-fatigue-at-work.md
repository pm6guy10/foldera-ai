Decision fatigue at work rarely shows up as a dramatic failure. It shows up as drift. Threads stay open longer than they should, priorities blur together, and small decisions start consuming the same energy as important ones.

AI can help, but only if it does more than summarize information. Useful AI for decision fatigue should narrow the field, remove low-value noise, and prepare a clear move instead of asking you to keep choosing from ten possibilities.

Foldera is built with that standard in mind. It is meant to reduce work by making decisions easier to execute, not by giving you another surface to manage.
