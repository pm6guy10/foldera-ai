An email needs action when it affects something that will meaningfully change with delay. That could be a deadline, trust, a blocked dependency, or a decision waiting on your response.

The tricky part is that those signals are often not obvious from the subject line or unread state alone. Action-worthiness usually lives in surrounding context, not just the message itself.
