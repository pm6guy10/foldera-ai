# Customer Push Checklist

- Open production in a real signed-out browser once after deploy and click the `/pricing` CTA to confirm it lands on `/start`.
- Open production in a real signed-in browser once after deploy and confirm `/dashboard` and `/dashboard/settings` render with the expected authenticated chrome.
- In a signed-in production session, open the dashboard command palette with `Cmd/Ctrl+K`, search for `settings`, and press Enter to confirm the search flow works end to end.
