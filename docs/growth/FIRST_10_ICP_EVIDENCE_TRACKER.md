## Locked ICP Hypothesis

A cognitively overloaded professional who loses work context across real sources and needs one safe re-entry move without rebuilding the whole day manually.

This ICP can narrow only from owner-approved, manually collected evidence. No invented persona, Brandon alias, fake beta user, or internal mock can count as real customer evidence.

Enforcement:

- Required tracker field: `ICP fit notes`.
- Verdict taxonomy: `WRONG_ICP` and `REJECTS_NORTH_STAR` must be available for negative or off-target evidence.
- First-10 batch stop rule: repeated wrong-ICP or rejection evidence stops the batch.

## Verdict Taxonomy

Use exactly these evidence verdicts:

- `SUPPORTS_NORTH_STAR`: The record ties a real workday reconstruction-tax pain to Foldera's one grounded re-entry move.
- `WEAK_SIGNAL`: The record shows possible interest, but pain, fit, proof, or urgency is too vague to support a claim.
- `REJECTS_NORTH_STAR`: The record shows the ICP does not value the Workday Presence Layer promise or one safe re-entry move.
- `WRONG_ICP`: The person, community, or conversation is not the locked ICP.
- `CLAIM_NOT_ALLOWED`: The record would require an unsupported public, connector, enterprise, pricing, Slack, Teams, email, calendar, automation, or scale claim.
- `NEEDS_NON_OWNER_PROOF`: The record is plausible, but needs proof from a non-owner before it can support product or public direction.
- `NO_ACTION_SAFE`: The record does not justify a reply, next issue, claim change, or product action.

Enforcement:

- Required tracker field: `Verdict`.
- First-10 batch stop rule blocks continued Stage 1 collection after repeated rejection or wrong-ICP evidence.
- Claim-control section blocks public claim expansion from a single record.

## Claim Control

Every evidence entry must classify public-claims impact as exactly one of:

- claim strengthened
- claim unchanged
- claim weakened
- claim forbidden

No public-site, pricing, connector, enterprise, Slack, Teams, email, calendar, automation, or scale claim may change from this tracker unless a future GitHub issue explicitly authorizes that change.

Claim impact rules:

- `claim strengthened` means the evidence may support a future issue for demo/readiness copy, but does not change public copy by itself.
- `claim unchanged` means the evidence does not affect current public claims.
- `claim weakened` means the claim should be treated as less safe until future evidence repairs it.
- `claim forbidden` means the tracker blocks expansion or repetition of that claim unless future proof changes source truth.

Enforcement:

- Required tracker field: `Public-claims impact`.
- Future public-site, pricing, connector, enterprise, Slack, Teams, email, calendar, automation, or scale changes require their own GitHub issue.
- PR changed-file review blocks public-site/product changes in issue #159.

## First-10 Batch Stop Rule

Stop the first-10 batch when any of these happens:

- 10 records exist.
- 3 consecutive `WRONG_ICP` / `REJECTS_NORTH_STAR` records occur.
- No real pain evidence exists after 10 hand-selected attempts.
- Evidence proves the value only works when Brandon narrates it.

When the batch stops, do not continue collecting as a growth campaign. Classify the result, write one recommended next issue if justified, or write `NO_ACTION_SAFE`.

Enforcement:

- Required tracker field: `Verdict`.
- Required tracker field: `Next manual action`.
- First-10 placeholders stop at 10 rows.
